About Hot-Pot World

Bringing authentic hot pot experience to your table

Our Story

Founded in 2020, Hot-Pot World was born from a passion for authentic Chinese hot pot cuisine. Our founders, having traveled extensively throughout China, fell in love with the communal dining experience and rich flavors of traditional hot pot.

We believe that hot pot is more than just a meal - it's a social experience that brings people together. Our mission is to share this wonderful tradition with our community while maintaining the highest standards of quality and authenticity.
